Research

I am interested star-forming galaxies and active galaxies in the high-redshift and local Universe, the extragalactic background light, and using extragalactic surveys to test various predictions of semi-analytic galaxy evolution models.

Select the research areas below to toggle details of my research programmes.

  • Far-infrared and submm extragalactic surveys
  • Space telescopes
  • Extragalactic near/mid-infrared astronomy
  • Cosmology
    • Science Associate on the ESA Planck High Frequency Instrument
    • Using strong gravitational lensing to constrain dark matter density profiles and cosmological parameters (part of the Herschel ATLAS survey)
