Current external roles

  • 2019-21: Chair, Scientific Council, Centre de Donnees astronomiques de Strasbourg
  • 2020-22: Chair, Advisory Board, REINFORCE Horizon 2020 project
  • 2019-2022: Lead, ECO work package of ESCAPE Horizon 2020 project
  • 2018-21: Chair of STFC Astronomy Advisory Panel (AAP) and lead of national community consultation in 2018. (AAP member 2015-18)
  • 2018-21: UK ALMA Oversight Committee member
  • 2019-20: President, Society for Popular Astronomy (Vice-President in 2018 and 2021)
  • 2017-present: STFC Food Network+ (Technology Champion 2017-19; co-I, SFN+ 2.0 from 2020)
  • 2017-present: Recruitment lead and executive team member for DISCnet, STFC Centre for Doctoral Training in Data-Intensive Science
  • 2015-present: Associate Editor, Publications of the Astronomical Society of Australia

Biography: Employment and Education

  • Professor of Astronomy at the Open University, 2016-present
  • Head of Astronomy Discipline at the Open University, 2010-2016
  • Reader in Cosmology at the Open University, 2010-2016
  • Senior Lecturer in Astrophysics at the Open University, from March 2006 to June 2010
  • Senior Lecturer in Astrophysics at the University of Kent, from September 2005 to February 2006
  • Lecturer in Space Science at the University of Kent, from March 2001 to September 2005
  • Post-doctoral research fellow at Imperial College London, from November 1995 to March 2001.
  • 1992-1995: D.Phil. (Oxon), title: Quasars and Galaxies at Early Cosmic Epochs
  • 1989-1992: B.Sc. first class honours in Physics, Bristol University

I am also a Fellow of the Institute of Physics, a Fellow of the Higher Education Academy, a Fellow of the Royal Astronomical Society and a Chartered Physicist.

I also passed the Postgraduate Certificate in Higher Education. This higher education teaching qualification, equivalent to one-third of an M.Sc., was and still is a requirement for new lecturers at the University of Kent.
